# Privacy Policy for Hotspot Weather

Last updated: April 2026

Hotspot Weather ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect the following data:

* Location data (latitude and longitude) – only when you choose to add a hotspot or use your current location.
* Saved hotspots – stored locally on your device.
* Weather data – retrieved from third-party weather services.

## How We Use Your Data

* To display weather forecasts for selected locations.
* To save your favorite locations (hotspots) on your device.

## Data Storage

* All saved locations are stored locally on your device.
* We do not store personal data on external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses external APIs such as weather providers to fetch weather data. These services may process location data according to their own privacy policies.

## Permissions

The app may request:

* Location access (to detect your current position)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data.
